DeerFlow-CN 是什么? | What is DeerFlow-CN?

想象一下,你有一位超级聪明的研究助手,能够在几分钟内帮你深度调研任何话题——从技术分析到市场研究,从学术探索到趋势洞察。这就是 DeerFlow-CN。

Imagine you have a super-smart research assistant who can help you deeply investigate any topic within minutes — from technical analysis to market research, from academic exploration to trend insights. That's DeerFlow-CN.

它不仅能生成详细的研究报告,还能将结果转换成 PPT 演示文稿 或 播客音频。

It can not only generate detailed research reports, but also convert results into PPT presentations or podcast audio.

核心洞察 | Key Insight

这种"分工协作"的模式是软件工程中最重要的理念之一,叫做 关注点分离(Separation of Concerns)。每个"角色"只专注做好一件事,整体系统就会既强大又易维护。

This "division of labor" pattern is one of the most important concepts in software engineering, called Separation of Concerns. Each "role" focuses on doing one thing well, making the overall system both powerful and easy to maintain.

什么是"智能体"? | What is an "Agent"?

把 智能体 想象成一个虚拟员工。它有特定的职责,能够使用工具,并且知道何时需要其他智能体的帮助。

Think of an agent as a virtual employee. It has specific responsibilities, can use tools, and knows when it needs help from other agents.

DeerFlow-CN 有五个核心智能体,就像一个迷你研究公司:

DeerFlow-CN has five core agents, like a mini research company:

五大角色 | The Five Roles

🎯

协调员 Coordinator

理解你的需求,决定是深度研究还是简单聊天,像项目经理一样协调整个团队。
Understands your needs, decides whether to do deep research or simple chat, coordinates the whole team like a project manager.

📋

规划师 Planner

制定研究计划,把大问题拆分成可执行的小步骤,像战略顾问。
Creates research plans, breaks big questions into executable steps, like a strategy consultant.

🔍

研究员 Researcher

执行具体的搜索和信息收集,调用搜索引擎、网页爬虫等工具。
Executes specific searches and information gathering, uses search engines, web crawlers, and other tools.

📝

报告员 Reporter

整合所有研究成果,撰写结构清晰、内容详实的最终报告。
Integrates all research findings, writes clear and detailed final reports.

💻

编码员 Coder

当需要运行代码验证或数据处理时,执行 Python 代码并返回结果。
Executes Python code for verification or data processing when needed, returns results.

好奇心探索 | Curiosity Corner

bind_tools 的意思是"给这个 AI 模型安装工具"。就像给工人配备电钻、锯子一样,我们给智能体配备搜索引擎、网页爬虫等工具。

bind_tools means "install tools for this AI model". Like equipping a worker with drills and saws, we equip agents with search engines, web crawlers, and other tools.
